The Best Movies According to Michael

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Die Hard

This is a classic action movie that I can watch, and have, watched over 1000 times. Also counts as a Christmas movie.

Dredd

Fantastic dark/sci-fi/dystopian gritty cop movie. As of writing this I just noticed how similar it and Die Hard are.

300

Fantastic action movie adaptation of an a-historic graphic novel.
